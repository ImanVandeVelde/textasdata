Mary Wollstonecraft Shelley - Frankenstein

Welcome to the mini digital edition of Mary Shelley's Frankenstein. Made as a project for Text as Data II, taught at the University of Antwerp.

Mary Wollstonecraft Shelley was born at the end of the 18th century and became famous for her work as an English novelist. She was married to Percy Bysshe Shelley (see picture), who often edited Shelley's work, like in the case of Frankenstein. The manuscript here displayes a draft of Frankenstein, with editions both made by Mary and Percy Shelley. This assignment was only made possible thanks to the Bodleian Library and the Shelley Godwin Archive.
